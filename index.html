What is Decoding Genetics?

Decoding Genetics is a visual learning hub that turns dense molecular biology into cinematic, easy-to-follow animations. Instead of static textbook diagrams, you'll see DNA, proteins, and cellular processes move the way they actually do — making the science easier to picture, remember, and explain.

🧬

Built for Visual Learners

Every concept is paired with 3D and 2D animation, not just text and diagrams.

📚

No Prior Knowledge Needed

Lessons build from the basics, so you can start wherever you are.

🔬

Scientifically Reviewed

Content is guided by clinical genetics and molecular biology experts.
